Why Choose Hosanna Secondary School?

At Hosanna Secondary School, we provide an enriching educational environment that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and personal growth. Our students benefit from:

  • Qualified and dedicated teaching staff
  • Modern learning facilities and resources
  • Comprehensive co-curricular programs
  • Safe and supportive learning environment
  • Strong values-based education
  • Excellent academic performance record

Admission Process

Step 1: Application

Submit a completed application form along with the required documents:

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Copy of previous school reports (last two years)
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Recommendation letter from previous school
Download Application Form

Step 2: Entrance Examination

All applicants are required to sit for an entrance examination covering:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • General Science
  • General Knowledge

Examination dates are announced at the beginning of each admission cycle.

Step 3: Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with the admissions panel. Both the student and parents/guardians should attend.

Step 4: Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an admission letter outlining:

  • Offer details
  • Reporting requirements
  • Fee payment schedule
  • School rules and regulations

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Hosanna Secondary School offers several scholarship opportunities for deserving students:

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Awarded to students who demonstrate outstanding academic performance in our entrance examination.

Covers: 25%-100% of tuition fees

Sports Talent Scholarship

For students with exceptional talent in sports (football, netball, athletics).

Covers: 25%-50% of tuition fees

Need-Based Financial Aid

Available for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds who show academic potential.

Requires submission of financial documents and a written application.
