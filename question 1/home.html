About Our School

Hosanna Secondary School is a premier educational institution committed to providing holistic education that combines academic excellence with character development. Located in a serene environment conducive to learning, we offer both O-Level and A-Level education with modern facilities and experienced teaching staff.

Our student-centered approach ensures that each learner receives personalized attention to help them achieve their full potential. We pride ourselves on our diverse student body and inclusive community that celebrates different talents and abilities.

Our Motto

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal development and responsible citizenship in a dynamic world.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that produces well-rounded individuals who are innovative, morally upright, and capable of contributing positively to society.
