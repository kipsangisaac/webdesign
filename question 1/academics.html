Our Curriculum

Hosanna Secondary School offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines the national education requirements with innovative teaching methods. Our programs are designed to challenge students intellectually while providing them with practical skills for the modern world.

Education Levels

  • Ordinary Level (O-Level): 4-year program for students aged 13-16
  • Advanced Level (A-Level): 2-year program for students aged 17-18

Academic Departments

Science Department

Our science department is equipped with modern laboratories and offers courses in:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Mathematics
  • Computer Science

Humanities Department

The humanities department fosters critical thinking and cultural awareness through:

  • History
  • Geography
  • Religious Education
  • Literature in English

Languages Department

We emphasize communication skills and multilingual education with:

  • English Language
  • Kiswahili
  • French
  • German

Technical Department

Practical skills development is our focus with courses in:

  • Agriculture
  • Business Studies
  • Art and Design
  • Home Economics
